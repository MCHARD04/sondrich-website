Who We Are

The Company Profile

Sondrich Limited designs, fabricates and installs premium European aluminium window and door systems — for homes, offices and developments across Tanzania.

Our Story

Built on precision, finished with care.

Sondrich Limited works with homeowners, architects, contractors and developers — from a single residential window to full building facades and shopfronts.

Our approach is simple: every project moves through the same disciplined process — consultation, precise measurement, fabrication, and installation by our own crew — so what's agreed on paper is exactly what gets handed over on site. We track every project through to a final sign-off, the same way we track the one on our home page: site visited, measured, fabricated, installed, and marked Status: Done.

European-Grade Materials

After-Installation Support

Our Approach

One team, start to finish.
